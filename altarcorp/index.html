FAQ

When does it release?+

ALTAR CORP is planned for early 2027. The best way to be notified the moment it launches is to add it to your wishlist on Steam.

What platform is it on?+

PC (Windows) via Steam at launch. Both English and French are supported for interface, audio and subtitles.

Who is making it?+

ALTAR CORP is mainly developed by NOIR FLEUVE with the help of a small team for various tasks such as lore writing, art, music production...

How can I follow and help development?+

Wishlist on Steam for launch updates, join the Discord for behind-the-scenes and playtests, and subscribe to the Patreon for exclusive WIPs and early access to builds.

THE STORY

You play as Leyla, sent into Yerneth: an abandoned city once devoted to knowledge, now overrun by the Lowkihs: blind creatures with hyper-developed senses.

Sent under the guise of an extermination mission, Leyla gradually uncovers that the truth behind Yerneth and the ALTAR corporation that once controlled it is far more complex than she was told.

Shoot in sync.

The Lowkihs are blind, but they're not forgiving. While exploring the Altar Corp tower, fire in rhythm with the room's current track with the help of the animated crosshair to boost your score and earn better rewards.

Explore the city.

Yerneth has plenty to discover. Talk to locals, loot what you find, set up your own space, and play your favorite tracks as you wander through the city.
