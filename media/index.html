ABOUT ALTAR

Founded in 2021 by Noir Fleuve, ALTAR started as a French event collective. While the focus was mainly on music, the desire to create and connect through visuals was already there, brought to life through video projections during those events.

The project went through quieter phases, but the vision never disappeared. It kept evolving in the background, shaped by ongoing experiments with low-poly visuals.

In early 2026, this direction naturally led to posting daily on Instagram with new characters, which quickly started gaining traction.

Today, ALTAR VISION is growing into an online community, a clothing brand, a video game, and many other things you can be part of.
